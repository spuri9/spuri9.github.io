BS Computer Science

Georgia Institute of Technology, 2018

Hi! I am Surina Puri. I am a fourth year computer science major at Georgia Tech. I am facinated by the research in artificial intelligence, machine learning and their applications in the optimization in various fields, especially healthcare.

I am involved in operational research in medicine and healthcare at Georgia Tech. During summer 2017, I participated in NSF REU at Washington University in St. Louis through CRA-W’s Distributed Research Experiences for Undergraduates (DREU) program. In this program, I worked on algorithms to predict users’ interactions as they interact with a visual interface.

I aspire to pursue a career in research that lies at the frontiers of artificial intelligence and data science. Apart from research, I also enjoy playing piano and basketball. I am a member of Girls Who Code, where I teach programming to middle and high school girls.
