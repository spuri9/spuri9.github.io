Connected Car Infotainment Project

Ui/Ux Intern
Tata Elxsi
May 2015 - August 2015

As part of my UI/UX internship at Tata Elxsi Ltd. during summer 2015, I worked on Connected Car Infotainment features.

My project was divided into three stages:

  • To research and compile a report on current features of Connected Car Infotainment systems of various automobile companies
  • Survey about the features desired by the public in Connected Car Infotainment systems
  • To cconceptualize new In-Car apps and make wireframes for the same.

After successfully completing the first two stages, I conceptualized ‘Travel Together Application’, a navigation feature that helps to plan and implement group travel and helps to keep track of other friends/family on the road.

The Travel Together App Wireframes document below contains my final report which includes details wireframes and workflow of the Travel Together In-Car Infotainment Application.
