Prediction Accuracy of the Algorith for Each Task

As a Research Intern, I worked on Predictive Visualization project under Dr. Ottley.

Tackling problems through visualization requires humans to draw inferences from complex visual representations. However, the computer's role is typically limited once the data is displayed. Our research aims to further support humans by predicting their interactions.

My research focused on using clustering to create models of the data and analyze usersâ€™ mouse clicks to predict their interests. I used Python Scikit learn library to develop the prediction model. This model was evaluated by conducting an online user study and analyzing the users' mouse clicks. The model achieved high levels of accuracy in predicting users' interest. This result lays the foundation for developing next-generation visualizations that assist the users during their analysis tasks.
