Operations Research in Healthcare

Undergraduate Research Assistant
Center for Operations Research in Medicine and Healthcare, Georgia Tech
Jan 2017 - Present

I am pursing interdisciplinary research in Industrial and Systems Engineering department at Georgia Tech, under Dr. Eva K. Lee.
The Research project focuses on optimization of healthcare procedures and on early detection of diseases.

My roles include:

  • Visiting hospitals for observations/data collection
  • Using data analytics for analysis of diseases
This research is providing me insights into various healthcare problems and at the same time also making me think of how AI and machine learning can help to solve those problems. I am passionate to use my knowledge of computer science to develop intelligent systems to diagnose or predict diseases at an early stage and to efficiently monitor them.
